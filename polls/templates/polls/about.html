About & Concept

Let us make the concept clear by a fundamantal way of answering the Five Ws.
1. WHAT - What is Opinifyme! ? Opinify Me is a platform created for the sole purpose of informing the peeps out there like you. This involves informing about various topics in regards of what the majority of the people think of it . Moreover , not only you get to know about the opinions but also the opinions are concise , thus understanding and comprehending them beacomes easier than ever. Now , you must be thinking that this exercise is not a very fruitfull one or cant be put to any practical uses. Continue with me onto the next section on the second W , where I explain why the above doubt is invalid.
2. Why - Why should I visit Opinifyme! ? Well , certainly it a lot more worth than you think it is . Let me give you an example , how many times have you heard the following statement:-

"According to a research / study , it is proven that (some random true/untrue fact)".

Now , when we genrally hear this sort of stuff , most of us tend to believe whatever is told to us. And I am not claiming that any of those are false. Now , what I am trying to say is that rather than blindly following one single person's insights or credibilty , how about we try getting opinions from more enlightened peeps out there. This will not only help you avoid the partially correct/incorrect opinion on a matter , but also , help you form a confidence factor on your opinion since you have abackup source with you to prove. However,trust me when you have the correct knowledge , you simply cannot be challenged.
There is another theory I would like to share which I think lies perfectly in the WHY section since it gives one of the concrete reasons of bending towards other's opinion. This theory is backed up by a brilliant book The Wisdom Of Crowds by James Surowiecki. The crux of this book is "Why the Many Are Smarter than the Few". More and more the brains and perspectives the better and correct face of the concept is in front of you. Having said that ,getting the opinions and putting them to use is one half of the cycle. We need your opinion too in this billateral process, because we believe every person int the world has something to contribute. But one thing promised, that is the one and only thing we want from you, and ofcourse your name to welcome you back here. Last but not the least , according to an observation commonly known as 1% rule when refered to internet. It states that that only 1% of the users of a website actively create new content, while the other 99% of the participants only lurk. Participating in this simple useful activity also gets you out of the lurkers category and gets you in the category of the contributors. So keep Opinifying!

3. Who - Who should visit Opinifyme! ? Anyone , who knows how to communicate can and should help himself by using the platform. Knowledge and its correctness is always a keen factor when a peron is exposed to the external world. Therefore anyone with sufficient interaction with the world outside or with a curiosity of a child (not necessarily a child) can use this.

4. When - When should I visit Opinifyme! ? Now is a good time. You have reached and are reading the about page. Certainly now would be the best time to give it a try.
